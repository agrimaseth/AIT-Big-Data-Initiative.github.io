    Welcome

    The AIT Big Data Initiative, is a group of like minded individuals, loosely led by Chirag Nagpal, consisting of students from Second and Third Year of Engineering at Army Institute of Technology

    What We Do

    Currently, we are involved with the development of large scalable clusters, for massive data mining. We plan to utilise aggregated data for analytics and research. If the terms 'Machine Learning', 'Big Data', 'Web Intelligence' are familiar to you, we would be interested in hearing from you.
